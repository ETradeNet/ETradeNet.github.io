The total amount of cash plus margin equity that is available for withdrawal from this account.

If the account's margin equity has fallen below E*TRADE's minimum account equity requirement, this value is the amount of additional equity required.
NonMarginableSecuritiesAndOptions
The value of securities and options that are not marginable.
ShortReserve
The amount reserved to cover the short positions in the account.
TotalShortValue
The total value of securities that are held short in the account.
